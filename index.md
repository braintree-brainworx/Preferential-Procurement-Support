# Preferential Procurement
Ensure seamless compliance with South African B-BBEE legislation.
Track, report, and manage your procurement spend against designated supplier groups – effortlessly.

The Preferential Procurement extension categorises their suppliers and spending on procurement according to the Preferential Procurement section of the South African legislation on Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment.
